01 Brand Experience

Orchestrating 100+ Brand Experiences for Fortune 500 Clients

Scope: End-to-end event communications

02 Digital Campaign

Turning a Pandemic Into Brand Positivity Through Creative Wordplay

Client: Nine Nine Travels
Role: Concept & Copy
Platform: Social Media

The Challenge

During the Covid-19 lockdown, a travel brand needed to stay visible and emotionally relevant — without being able to sell travel. The challenge: how do you keep a travel brand alive when no one can travel?

My Approach

Instead of ignoring the situation, I leaned into it. I crafted a series of concepts that used travel-related wordplay to convey solidarity, hope, and unity — transforming the brand from a travel company into a community voice.

The Execution

Created multiple digital posts including "With U & I in UNION, we will stay strong and safe" (international flags concept), "WE ARE ONE IN THIS FLIGHT" (airplane window + mask visual), and "LOCK & UPGRADE" (car lockdown play). Each combined clever copy with strong visual direction.

The Impact

4+Viral concepts created
HighEngagement & shares
RetainedBrand visibility during zero-revenue period
03 Sales Enablement

Scripting Peak Performance for Pharmaceutical Sales Teams

Clients: Abbott, Glenmark, GroupM, Kotak
Role: Script Writer & Content Curator
Cadence: Quarterly & Yearly

The Challenge

Large pharmaceutical and financial services companies needed their salesforce teams to stay motivated through quarterly targets and year-end pushes. Generic motivational content wasn't cutting it — they needed content that was specific, strategic, and tied to measurable goals.

My Approach

I treated each motivational video not as "content" but as a strategic intervention. Every script was tailored to the specific quarter's challenges, the team's performance data, and the company's cultural language — making it feel personal, not corporate.

The Execution

Curated and scripted motivational video content on quarterly and yearly cycles. Each video combined emotional storytelling with data-driven narratives, goal-anchored messaging, and company-specific language that resonated with field teams.

04 Content Strategy

Leading a Full Website Content Overhaul for a Travel Brand

Client: 9ty9 Travels
Role: Content Strategy Lead
Scope: Complete website revamp

The Challenge

9ty9 Travels needed a complete website overhaul — not just a redesign, but a rethinking of how content served the user journey. The existing site had poor SEO visibility, inconsistent brand voice, and content that didn't convert browsers into bookers.

My Approach

I led the project from ideation to execution, starting with user journey mapping and competitive content analysis. I defined a content architecture that aligned with search intent, brand positioning, and conversion goals — then collaborated with designers and developers to bring it to life.

The Execution

Curated engaging, SEO-optimized content for every page. Worked closely with stakeholders to gather input, used user feedback to inform content choices, and coordinated with developers for seamless implementation. Ensured the final product aligned with the brand's tone, goals, and growth strategy.
