Hi, I'm Sowbhagya (but you can call me Soby).

I have over five years of experience as a copywriter, crafting words for everything from digital agencies and on-ground events to marketing campaigns and more. I began as a copywriting intern, diving into the world of advertising, and eventually earned a Bachelor's in Mass Media, a Master's in Advertising, and a Postgraduate degree in Marketing & Entrepreneurship from Trent University.

Throughout my journey, I've had the privilege of working with over 70+ clients across diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, automobiles, banking, FMCG, lifestyle, and more. What I love most is creating content that truly connects with people, sparks interest, and delivers results because, at the end of the day, it's all about making words work for you.
